# ♟️ 50 Authentic Chess Motivational Quotes
*A Complete Compendium of Grandmaster Insights on Discipline, Resilience, Strategy, and Artistry.*

## 🎯 On Finding the Best Move (Discipline & Focus)

1. **"When you see a good move, look for a better one."** — *Emanuel Lasker* (2nd World Champion)

2. **"The riposte to the question of how many moves a player can see ahead is 'one, the right one.'"** — *Popular Grandmaster Maxim* (Fine, 2014)

3. **"Tactics is knowing what to do when there is something to do; strategy is knowing what to do when there is nothing to do."** — *Savielly Tartakower*

4. **"You must calculate moves quickly and then interpret them coldly."** — *International Master Insight* (Fine, 2014)

5. **"First-class error occurs when you don’t check the opponent's options."** — *Garry Kasparov* (13th World Champion)

6. **"I don't believe in psychology. I believe in good moves."** — *Bobby Fischer* (11th World Champion)

7. **"Concentration must be absolute."** — *Bobby Fischer* (11th World Champion)

8. **"You have to have that edge, you have to have that confidence, you have to have that absolute belief that you're the best."** — *Magnus Carlsen* (16th World Champion)

9. **"Chess is the art of analysis."** — *Mikhail Botvinnik* (6th World Champion)

10. **"Help your pieces so they can help you."** — *Paul Morphy*

## 🔄 On Dealing with Bad Positions & Mistakes (Resilience)

11. **"No matter how good you get as a chess player, you're going to get in bad positions. And you have to deal with them, you can't just give up when you have them."** — *Elizabeth Spiegel* (Saha, 2022)

12. **"The blunders are all there on the board, waiting to be made."** — *Savielly Tartakower*

13. **"We need to deal with those failures the right way so we can continue to push boundaries."** — *Mindy / Chess Design Insight* (Saha, 2022)

14. **"You may learn much more from a game you lose than from a game you win."** — *José Raúl Capablanca* (3rd World Champion)

15. **"I look one move ahead... the best one."** — *José Raúl Capablanca* (3rd World Champion)

16. **"A passed pawn is a criminal, who should be kept under lock and key."** — *Aron Nimzowitsch*

17. **"The most powerful weapon in chess is to have the next move."** — *David Bronstein*

18. **"Confidence is very important. If you don't think you can win, you will take cowardly decisions."** — *Magnus Carlsen* (16th World Champion)

19. **"Nobody ever won a chess game by resigning."** — *Savielly Tartakower*

20. **"Some part of the mistake is always correct."** — *Savielly Tartakower*

## 🎨 On the Beauty & Artistry of Chess

31. **"Chess is a fortunate art form. [...] Its strength is in its interpretation. This phenomenon enables one repeatedly to reproduce beauty."** — *David Bronstein* (Olechnicki, 2020)

32. **"All artists are not chess players – all chess players are artists."** — *Marcel Duchamp* (Olechnicki, 2020)

33. **"The game of chess is the touchstone of the intellect."** — *Goethe* (Fine, 2014)

34. **"The bigger the sacrifice, the more beautiful."** — *Traditional Brilliancy Concept* (Olechnicki, 2020)

35. **"Chess is everything—art, science, and sport."** — *Anatoly Karpov* (12th World Champion)

36. **"Chess, like love, like music, has the power to make men happy."** — *Siegbert Tarrasch*

37. **"The beauty of a move lies not in its appearance, but in the thought behind it."** — *Aron Nimzowitsch*

38. **"There are two types of sacrifices: correct ones, and mine."** — *Mikhail Tal* (8th World Champion)

39. **"I will suffer like a dog, but I will make beautiful games."** — *Vasily Smyslov* (7th World Champion)

40. **"If your opponent wants a draw, take him into a deep dark forest where 2+2=5, and the path leading out is only wide enough for one."** — *Mikhail Tal* (8th World Champion)

42. **"In chess, as in corporate strategy, it is about improving strategic thinking and decision-making skills."** — *Anatoly Karpov & Luca Desiata* (Saha, 2022)
